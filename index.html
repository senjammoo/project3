THE FEED
Spring 2026

The Feed is an interactive web experience that simulates a social media platform — and then slowly breaks it apart.

As you scroll, content disappears. Accounts get flagged. Captions are redacted mid-sentence. The platform turns on the people using it — which is exactly what real platforms already do.

This project responds to the documented suppression of voices discussing immigration enforcement, Black maternal healthcare, gender-based violence, and political accountability. The jose_fl posts reference real events: Operation Metro Surge and the killings of Renée Good and Alex Pretti by federal agents in Minneapolis in January 2026. Both were US citizens. Both were filmed. Video contradicted official accounts in both cases.

This is for anyone who has ever posted something true and watched it quietly disappear.

Creator: Seneca Moore
